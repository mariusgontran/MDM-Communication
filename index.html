About Us

Welcome to MDM Communication, your trusted partner in cutting-edge communication and security solutions. Formerly known as Marmic Communications, we have been a beacon of excellence in the industry since our establishment in 2008. With over 15 years of dedicated experience, we take pride in serving the diverse communication needs of Melbourne.

At MDM Communication, we are experts in the intricate world of communication systems. We specialize in the installation and servicing of cutting-edge security solutions, including CCTV systems, access control systems, and intercoms. Our team brings a wealth of experience to every project, ensuring that our clients receive top-notch service and solutions tailored to their unique needs.

Our services extend to the entire metro area, with a special focus on the CBD and inner suburbs. Whether you are a homeowner, business owner, or property manager, we are here to enhance your security and communication systems, providing peace of mind and convenience.
