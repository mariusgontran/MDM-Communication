About Us

Keeping you secured and connected since 2008

MDM Communication, formerly Marmic Communications established in 2008, has over 15 years of experience in communication systems. Specializing in Melbourne's CBD and inner suburbs, we excel in installing and servicing security systems, including CCTV, access control, and intercoms. Our team, with extensive expertise, provides tailored solutions for residential and commercial needs.

Choose MDM for cutting-edge intercoms that enhance security and communication, advanced access control systems for fortified protection, and state-of-the-art CCTV systems ensuring 24/7 surveillance and remote monitoring. Trust us to bring peace of mind to your property with our reliable and customized security solutions.
